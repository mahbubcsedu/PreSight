Some guidleines for identifyng the dimensions of sidewalk tile -
If there are two sidewalk tiles occupying the total width of a sidewalk then the width of each sidealk tile is close to 48 inches.
Acording to guidelines by ADA, no sidewalk can have width less than 48 inches.
Length of sidewalk is proportional to its width. (Mostly, longer than the width)
